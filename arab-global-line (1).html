Container Types

Our Container Fleet

We handle all standard ISO container sizes. Choose the right container for your cargo type and volume.

20' STANDARD
20 Feet Standard
5.9m × 2.35m × 2.39m — 33 CBM

Ideal for dense cargo, heavy goods, and smaller shipments. The most common container worldwide — perfect for machinery, chemicals, and consumer goods.

40' STANDARD
40 Feet Standard
12.03m × 2.35m × 2.39m — 67 CBM

Best value for full container loads. Double the volume of a 20ft for larger shipments. Ideal for furniture, textiles, automotive parts, and general cargo.

40' HIGH CUBE
40 Feet High Cube
12.03m × 2.35m × 2.70m — 76 CBM

Extra 30cm height for light, voluminous cargo. Perfect for furniture, machinery, oversized goods, and any cargo needing maximum cubic volume.
